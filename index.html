Privacy

Your data lives in two places, and only two places:

  1. This browser's local storage on the device you're using right now.
  2. The Google Sheet you created and own. No one else has access to it unless you share it.

What we do not store: nothing on any server we run. There is no central database. This app is static HTML hosted on GitHub Pages, and the only thing it can do is talk to your own Apps Script.

What you are responsible for: keeping your Apps Script URL secret (treat it like a password), and securing the device this app runs on (browser lock, device passcode).

To delete your data: click Reset local data above to wipe the browser cache, then delete your Google Sheet to wipe the cloud copy. We have nothing to delete on our end.
